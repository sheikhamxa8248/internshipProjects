Chicago, officially known as the City of Chicago, is:

  • The most populous city in the U.S. state of Illinois,
  • The most populous city in the Midwestern United States with an estimated population of 2,693,976 in 2019.
  • The county seat of Cook County, the second-most-populous county in the US.

It is also the principal city of the Chicago metropolitan area, often referred to as Chicagoland. At nearly 10 million people, the metropolitan area is the third most populous in the United States.
